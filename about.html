About

Hey, I'm Todd. I learned about HTML in High School and thought I would go into Computer Science in college and begin my career. Life took me down a different path, but here I am today learning about Web Design once again in my 30's. I've been teaching myself through online videos, books, and mozilla developer tools since March, 2017. I'm a husband, father, and huge Clemson fan! My goal is to use this site to show off my portfolio, land a job as a Web Developer, and to offer my services to friends and family.
